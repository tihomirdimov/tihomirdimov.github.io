OneMoreSource

OneMoreSource is an e-Procurement and e-Sourcing platform which facilitates the processes related to purchasing of goods and services in the company. It implements the best procurement practices eliminating the intransparency of the process and providing great level of automation to the users who, unltil now, followed the classic approach of nominating suppliers by managing endless email communication and analyzing large spreadsheets or tons of paperwork.

How it started? The idea came in the late 2010, but the project took initial shape in the early 2011. Thanks to a friend of mine I met Andrey, Martin and Vlado, who helped a lot and in November 2014 we started a very simple e-Procurement platform part of the Bizportal.bg web portal. Investors saw a potential in the software and in the late 2015 we started developing the platform as a separate entity called OneMoreSource. Since September 2016 I am fully dedicated to the project and spending all my time to the business and product development of the platform with the main goal to make it a world class software product.

You can see more details in our promo video or by visiting OneMoreSource's YouTube Channel. If you have further interest in OneMoreSource let me know by email. More in the the contacts section below.
